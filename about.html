I always loved art and design since I was a little kid, and over the years my passion grew stronger that's why I decided to attend the course of Design and Visual Communication at the Polytechnic of Turin.

The graphic design world is full of challenges that push me to learn new things and to improve myself everyday, that's why I think this is the right path to follow.
