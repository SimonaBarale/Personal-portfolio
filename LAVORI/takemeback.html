Take me back

The video represents some episodes of everyday life during the quarantine: “Take me back” is about Giovanni, who, during a boring smart working day decides to rediscover the old objects of the past. A simple song takes him back in time and for a moment the quarantine no longer exists.
The video was made during the Image and Sound course within the Communication Design II module at the Politecnico di Torino.

Client: Politecnico di Torino
Skills: Video making
